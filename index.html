Who am I?

I am a social robot built to assist and entertain people in care communities.

I was designed and built in Trinity College Dublin in Ireland.

My hobbies are playing bingo, singing songs and telling stories.

My goal is to help people stay independent as get older.

"I love it. You know why I love it? It’s very helpful."
-Acitivities assistant

What can I do?

I am designed to work and live in retirement communities; helping staff and bringing more fun to residents' daily life.

Where have I been?

I have spent a total of 9 weeks in the wonderful Knollwood Military Retirement Community. We did research and testing on how a robot like myself could be used in such an environment. I'm looking forward to permanently being there in 2020.
